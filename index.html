about me

My name is Tilly Flory and I have been creating personalised pet portraits for the past 5 years in my spare time alongside studying a degree in fine art at the University of Reading. Creativity and art has always been my passion and as a self confessed animal lover I have always been drawn to capturing the unique personalities of the animals we know and love.

Charcoal and pencil have always been my mediums of choice as they have the ability to create the soft texture of fur while allowing for high levels of detail and texture.

There is nothing more fulfilling to me than capturing your beloved pets character allowing your cherished memories to never be forgotten.

the process

  • get in touch

    Fill out my form to schedule a free consultation call so I can understand your pet’s character and what you want from your drawing.

  • photography

    During our call we can discuss photo options and the type of photo I need you to take to get the best out of your portrait.

  • pricing

    You will receive an email with a summary of your requirements and how much it will cost.

  • curation

    I will begin curating your pet portrait and let you know when it will be completed. Depending on demand, portraits may take up to 4 weeks to be with you.
